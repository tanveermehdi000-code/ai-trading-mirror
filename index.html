FAQ

Questions traders ask us.

Everything you need to know before joining.

What trading journals does ATM support? ↓
We support any CSV export from platforms like Tradovate, TradeStation, Thinkorswim, MT4/MT5, Interactive Brokers, Webull, and more. If you can export a CSV with date, symbol, entry, exit and result — we can analyze it.
Is my trading data safe and private? ↓
Yes. Your trade data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. We only use it to generate your personal discipline report. You can delete your data at any time from your account settings.
How is this different from a regular trade journal? ↓
Regular journals show you what happened. ATM tells you WHY it happened and what it's costing you. It detects behavioral patterns like revenge trading, FOMO entries, and overtrading automatically — no manual tagging required.

Does it work for all markets — stocks, forex, crypto, futures? ↓
Yes. ATM analyzes behavior, not the market itself. Whether you trade equities, forex, crypto, or futures — the behavioral patterns and discipline scoring work exactly the same way.
